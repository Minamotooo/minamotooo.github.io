BUET CSE Fest 2026 | March 2026

Tackled low-resource Natural Language Processing for Bengali long-form audio across two tracks: for ASR, built WhisperAlign — a word-boundary-aware chunker using Whisper's cross-attention alignment heads — and fine-tuned a Whisper-medium Bengali ASR model; for diarization, applied Machine Learning domain adaptation by fine-tuning Pyannote's segmentation head on Bengali conversational speech, paired with a Dual-VAD intersection for noise-robust boundary detection.

1st Runner Up ResearchSpark

1st Runner Up, ResearchSpark 3 Minute Thesis Competition

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) | April 2026

Presented BanglaHateCorpus — a unified Bangla hate speech dataset of 168K instances from 8 social media platforms, with a verification pipeline combining human-linguist review and LLM-as-judge scoring. Presented BanHateFormer, a lightweight Transformer with Mixture-of-Experts feed-forward layers targeting 91.33% F1-score at 94× smaller scale than BanglaBERT, toward deployable NLP-based content moderation for 242 million Bangla speakers.

2nd Runners Up BUET CSE Fest 2024

2nd Runners Up, Research Poster Presentation

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) | October 2024

Designed a real-time multimodal threat detection system that cross-validates social media text with government CCTV footage. The pipeline combines NLP-based rumor classification, CNN+RNN video frame analysis, and a confidence-scoring fusion module to categorize incidents into 16 threat classes — intersecting Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Multimodal AI for public safety.

1st Runner Up HULT Prize

1st Runner Up, HULT Prize at BUET

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) | February 2026

Pitched ATLAS, an AI-powered adaptive tutoring platform targeting 24 million underserved Bangladeshi students. The proposed system integrates handwritten script OCR via Vision Transformers, Bayesian Knowledge Tracing for personalized learning paths, and BanglaBERT-based NLP grading — a multimodal machine learning approach to democratizing quality education.
