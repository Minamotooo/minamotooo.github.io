MemoryLane
Framed grayscale-to-color mapping as a conditional image generation problem in the CIELab color space, building a ConvNeXt-Base encoder–U-Net decoder with a PatchGAN discriminator; addressed the gray-mean bias endemic to deep colorization models via colorfulness-filtered training data (~518K Places365 images) and a composite perceptual + adversarial loss (VGG + PatchGAN) — surpassing ground-truth colorfulness (40.9 vs. 36.9) and generalizing to real family photographs.
